About this item

Age : 0 Months+ Upto 22kg

Let us introduce NUBI 2 - a slim, elegant stroller that will improve your vacation. This stroller does not compromise: NUBI 2 automatically folds itself (one move is enough!), and in addition it is extremely light and maneuverable, and you can push it with one hand. The NUBI 2 stroller has a smooth and silent backrest adjustment, an adjustable footrest, a spacious seat, and a large, waterproof hood with a UPF50+ filter and a ventilation panel hidden under the fabric. The wheels made of abrasion-resistant PU are responsible for a smooth ride. The front ones rotate 360 degrees, making the stroller extremely manoeuvrable.
The stroller has a five-point safety harness that can be adjusted to the newborn. They are stiffened, thanks to which fastening the baby will take you only a moment. As standard, the stroller is equipped with a roomy basket under the seat and a number of additional amenities that allow for comfortable holidays with a child.

  • It folds itself - you just need one move
  • Light and compact - always ready to go
  • Ergonomic, stiffened safety harness - comfortable and safe
  • Up to 24 kg - the ability to adjust the stroller to the growing child
  • Waterproof hood with UPF50+ filter, opening ventilation panel, mosquito net hidden in the footrest
